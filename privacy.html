Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 2025

DailyBrain is committed to protecting your privacy. This page explains what information we collect and how we use it.

What We Collect

  • Game Data: We store your puzzle progress and results in your browser using local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not shared with anyone.
  • Contact Form: If you use the contact form, your name, email, and message are sent securely to us via a third-party service (such as Formspree). Your email address is not stored on our website.

How We Use Your Information

  • Game Data: Used only to keep track of your progress and stats. You can clear this data anytime by clearing your browser's local storage.
  • Contact Form: Your message is used only to respond to your inquiry. We do not sell or share your information.

Cookies & Analytics

We do not use cookies or analytics trackers on this site.

Your Rights

You can request deletion of any messages you send us via the contact form. For game data, simply clear your browser's local storage.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us.
